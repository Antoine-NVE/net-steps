Game Rules

Setup

  • 1. Each player selects a pawn and places it at the starting point of the board.
  • 2. Shuffle the question cards, each containing one question with three difficulty levels: easy, medium, and hard.
  • 3. Explain the different types of spaces on the board:
    • Question Square: The player answers a question based on the difficulty they choose.
    • Ladder Square: Allows the player to skip ahead to a higher space.
    • Virus Square: Forces the player to move backward.
    • Mystery Square: The player draws a Mystery Card and follows its instructions.
  • 4. Determine the turn order: the youngest player starts, and the game continues in a clockwise direction.

Gameplay

  1. Players take turns rolling the die to advance on the board.
  2. When a player lands on a Question Square:
    • They must choose a difficulty level: easy, medium, or hard.
    • The chosen difficulty determines the number of answer choices visible on the card:
      • Easy: The player sees 2 answer choices.
      • Medium: The player sees 4 answer choices (all possible answers).
      • Hard: No answer choices are provided; the player must respond without hints.
    • Correct answers allow the player to move forward:
      • Easy: Advance 1 space.
      • Medium: Advance 2 spaces.
      • Hard: Advance 3 spaces.
    • Incorrect answers result in no additional movement.
  3. Reaching the Finish Line:
    • Players must land exactly on the final square to win the game.
    • If a roll would take them past the finish line, they must "bounce back" by moving forward to the finish and then backward for the remaining steps.
    • Example: If a player is on square 98 and rolls a 3, they would move to square 99, then 100, and back to 99.
  4. Special spaces add twists to the game:
    • Ladder Square: Jump to a higher space on the board.
    • Virus Square: Move backward.
    • Mystery Square: Draw a Mystery Card and follow its effect, which may be positive or negative.

Objective

The first player to land exactly on the final square wins the game.

Question Square Summary

  • Easy: 2 answer choices, advance 1 space if correct.
  • Medium: 4 answer choices, advance 2 spaces if correct.
  • Hard: No answer choices, advance 3 spaces if correct.
